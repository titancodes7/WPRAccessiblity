Helpful Resources

We understand that seeking mental health support can feel overwhelming. This page connects you with trusted resources and four key places where you can find professional help, support, and guidance.

Service Phone Website Description
National Suicide Prevention Lifeline (USA) 988 988lifeline.org 24/7, free and confidential support for people in distress.

Lifeline (Australia) 13 11 14 lifeline.org.au Support for anyone experiencing emotional distress, anytime.
Mental Health America N/A mhanational.org Resources, screenings, and tools for mental wellness.

Practical Tips

  • Practice deep breathing: Inhale for 4 seconds, hold, exhale for 6.
  • Keep a short daily journal to process thoughts and feelings.
  • Try the 5‑4‑3‑2‑1 grounding technique if feeling anxious: Name 5 things you see, 4 you can touch, 3 you hear, 2 you smell, and 1 you taste.

Apps and Tools

  • Calm – Meditation, relaxation, and sleep aid.
  • Headspace – Daily mindfulness and stress management.
  • Moodpath – Mental health tracking and resources.

Wellness Resources

  • Sleep Hygiene Tips – Improve rest and recovery.
  • Regular light exercise such as walking or yoga to boost mood.
  • Balanced nutrition: Include fresh fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.

Privacy Information

Your privacy matters to us. Any contact forms you submit are securely stored and never shared with third parties without your consent.

Remember: Reach out to one of these trusted places today — you don’t have to face this alone.
