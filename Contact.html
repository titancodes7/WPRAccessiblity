Accessibility Information:

Our facility is fully wheelchair accessible, with support rails in restrooms and step-free entry. We also offer a hearing loop system in all meeting rooms and printed materials in braille upon request.

Common Questions:

Do you charge for consultations?

Our initial 20-minute phone consultation is free. Any follow-up sessions will have a standard booking fee, which depends on the type of service you require.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we work with most major insurance providers. We recommend contacting your provider directly to confirm your coverage for mental health services.

How soon can I book an appointment?

Appointment availability depends on the service and specialist requested, but generally we can offer you a slot within 7–10 working days.

Can I change or cancel my appointment?

Yes, though we request at least 24 hours’ notice for cancellations or changes to avoid a late cancellation fee.
